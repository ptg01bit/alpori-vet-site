About Us

Founded in 2015, Aloropi Veterinary Center has become a trusted destination for pet owners seeking compassionate and professional veterinary care. Our mission is simple: to enhance the health and happiness of every animal that walks through our doors.

We leverage cutting‑edge technology and evidence‑based medicine, all while maintaining a gentle, personalized approach for each pet.
